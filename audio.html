Rhythm Changes

This is a rhythm changes project that I did for a jazz harmony class.

"I Hate LaGuardia HS - Chris Lah"

This song is originally a very well-known Korean song, "I Miss You" by Kim Bum Soo.
I played the piano arrangement from akbobada.com. I initially asked my brother to sing, and then decided to change up the lyrics, which talk about him in agony of his high school. The lyrics aren't intended to create any harm towards the school.

Short Cover of Sam Kim's "Your Song"

I really liked this song at one point during the Fall 2017 semester, so I decided to do a quick cover using the limited resources I had. I used an akai 25-key keyboard, which isn't really meant to be used for actual keyboard playing.

Chopin Waltz Op. 64 No. 3

This was my jury piece, and I recorded this during one of my practice times. I had a lot of trouble balancing my hands, since the melody had to stand out, and with the last ~1 or 2 minutes. In the end, my jury turned out well! :)
